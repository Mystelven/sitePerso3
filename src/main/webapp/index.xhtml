A little bit about me


Hi ! I'm Valentin Montmirail, I was born September 19, 1991.

I have always loved computers, having started in 1996 with a Windows 95,
I had already started to understand how things worked inside.
So I made all my studies for this purpose.

I first started with a Bachelor specialty Mathematics, I got with honors.
Then I continued with a DUT in Computer Science, held at IUT de Lens.

From 2012 to 2015, I've done my studies in Ecole Polytechnique de l'Université de Tours, to get my engineering degree.

And now, from 2015 to 2018, I will do my Ph.D in Artificial Intelligence on the topic of modal logic and modal satisfiability problems.
With Daniel Le Berre as my thesis director.
